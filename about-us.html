Vision & Mission

Vision
To be the school flying with wings towards excellence providing actual life experience for students of all ages and culture, to mold our students to be compassionate, progressive and professionally successful human beings responsive to world culture and heritage, in an environment of sharing and caring. We believe that every child has hidden talents and if nurtured in a conducive environment, it will help his/her personality to bloom to the desired level of their chosen professional field. We strive to provide a lively, warm and purposeful atmosphere for the pursuit of overall development and excellence.
Mission
To provide a positive, vibrant environment for quality education, with a scientific temper, adaptive to the changing needs of time, to provide a teaching and learning processes which is enriched with purposeful and deliberate experiences, To encourage children to think independently and creatively, to be passionate about what they wish to do, to value excellence, to ensure all round development of the child and expose him/her to a wide spectrum of co-cultural learning, to garner the best values from our rich and great Indian culture and tradition and other world cultures, also to expose the children to experience and feel proud of our Indian social and spiritual heritage ,creating genuine pride and respect for their country.
