Correspondent Message

Correspondent & Secretary’s Message

Malar Public School Started in  the year 2017-June is a warm and welcoming learning community, committed to creating the best possible educational experience for every child who comes to us. We aim to enable all children to reach their full potential.

We have a talented, dedicated, caring team of staff, everyone works with conviction tries hard to ensure that the abilities of the children in our care are nurtured and carefully developed. Our teachers and teaching assistants are experienced, and work as a team to make the school a very special place, every day, for your child.

We aim to provide a broad, well balanced and relevant curriculum. We are well aware that the need of the hour is not just academic input alone but career supportive skill enhancement. So we use every opportunity to support encourage and develop the inherent skill in every child.  We encourage positive social behavior, emphasizing respect for others. We believe that every child has the right to work in a calm, orderly, safe and secure environment. Malar Public School provides a stimulating and enriched environment so that the children can enjoy all aspects of their learning in the academic and non academic areas. Sports and games is also an integral and equally important part of the curriculum.

The children here are given ample opportunities to express themselves, – for example through class discussions and other skill oriented programmes and activities. We listen to the  children’s views also to make our school a place that children value and one that they want to come to.

Our school is housed in a superb building, offering all the learning facilities for children and teachers. We look forward to working with you as partners in your child’s education.

Wishing you all the very best...

Sri. R.Kandasamy

Correspondent
About Us
Malar public School is setup in the quiet and serene background of Paramathi in Namakkal dt. The colourful past of the Malar Institutions is a pride to this area. The school functions with its roots strongly kept on good social values. We welcome everyone with the open mind to be with us in this journey of enjoyable education with a scientific temper.
